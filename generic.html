Vision and Objectives

Our founding Values

VISION

To provide an open and multivibrant inter-disciplinary platform wherein students and faculties can collaborate, interact and work together in order to promote a healthy innovative research environment for academic growth, incubation and excellence.

Objectives

  • To provide an innovative research work exposure and project development opportunities to students and faculties through a common platform for all the departments of NIT Hamirpur.
  • They can float and put up their project proposal, research interests and innovative ideas. All the willing applicants sharing common interests can collaborate and work together in implementing project and research endeavours.
  • Along with project implemetation, assistance regarding writing a research paper, report, articles or any other publication work would be provided.
  • Contacting angel firms/institutes who can contribute towards research cause by providing a platform where project/research work done by the innovative minds of the college can be show- cased or even practically implemented to solve or tackle real time problems.
  • To promote a sound engineering and technical environment in the college by providing short term (winter break) and long term(summer break) internship opportunities especially for second and third year students under the guidance of our interested and dedicated faculty.

    • Integration with current research environment

    To provide a environment in college to promote and bolster research endeavours and convert some unconventional ideas into market-ready materials.
